  <div class="text" id="zoom_period_plot">
   <p> Zoom Period Plot </p> 
   <p><img src="images/zoom_period_plot.png"> </p> 
   <ul>
    <li>
     Use this plot to interactively investigate discrete water-quality information that has been 
     collected at a site.
    </li>
    <li>
     Hovering the cursor over a data point will toggle a popup that will show discrete water-quality 
     information about the selected measurement.
    </li>
    <li>
     To zoom to a shorter period, left-click and drag over the period of interest in either the
     upper or lower graph.
    </li>
   </ul>
  </div>

  <hr></hr>

  <div class="text" id="period_selected_plot">
   <p> Period Selected Plot </p> 
   <p><img src="images/period_selected_plot.png"> </p> 
   <ul>
    <li>
     Use this plot to manage the zoom period plot showing discrete water-quality information that has been 
     collected at a site.
    </li>
    <li>
     Double click within the boundaries of this plot to reset the starting and ending dates of the zoom
     period plot to the original starting and ending dates.
    </li>
   </ul>
  </div>
